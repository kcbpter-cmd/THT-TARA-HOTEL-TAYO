About Us

Our Story:Tara Hotel Tayo started as a simple idea between friends and family who shared a common dream to build a place that feels like home for both locals and travelers. We began with a small piece of land in our hometown. Instead of hiring big contractors, we worked with local carpenters, masons, and painters. The design was kept simple but warm inspired by Filipino culture, with a mix of traditional and modern touches. Most of the materials we used were locally sourced, and we made sure to support small businesses in the process. Every part of the hotel, from the front desk to the rooms, was built with teamwork, late nights, and a lot of hard work. It wasn’t easy. We faced delays, budget issues, and had to learn many things along the way. But step by step, Tara Hotel Tayo came to life. Today, we’re proud to say that this hotel was built not just with money, but with heart, community effort, and a shared dream to offer true Filipino hospitality.
